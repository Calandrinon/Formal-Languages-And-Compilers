- tokens are delimited by separators
- multiple consecutive separators can be replaced by a single separator
- classes of tokens:
	. identifiers
	. constants
	. reserved words |
	. separators     | predefined tokens
	. operators      |
	. can't be classified => lexical error

1. detect
2. classify
3. codify

while (not(eof)) do
	detect(token);
	if token is reserved word OR operator OR separator
		then genPIF(token, 0)
		else
		if token is identifier or constant
			then index = pos(token, ST);
			     genPIF(token, index)
			else message "Lexical error"
		endif
	endif
endwhile
